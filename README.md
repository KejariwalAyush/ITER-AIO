## Features

- Attendence
- Results
- Video Lectures (Can Play video on 3rd Party Players e.g., MX Player, VLC, etc)
- News & Notices about SOA & ITER
- Bunk Planning
- Academic Calender (Coming Soon)
- Study Materials 
- Switch Themes & Dark Mode
- Report a bug from within the App
